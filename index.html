    Purcell Huang

    Ph.D. Candidate in Statistical Machine Learning
    Department of Statistics, University of Oxford

    I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Oxford (opens new window), supervised by Yee Whye Teh and Tom Rainforth, and I am part of the OxCSML group.

    My recent research interests include meta learning, equivariance in deep learning, and they share the same goal of making deep learning data efficient. I am also increasingly passionate about the intersection of machine learning and science, including algorithms that draw inspiration from physics, mathematics, and using machine learning to accelerate scientific discovery in disciplines such as chemistry, biology and physics.

    I am funded by the Oxford-Tencent Collaboration on Large Scale Machine Learning project.
